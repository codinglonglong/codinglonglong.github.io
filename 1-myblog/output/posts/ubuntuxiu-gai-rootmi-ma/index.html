Ubuntu修改root密码

龙龙

2015-09-15 12:32

Source

Ubuntu修改root密码的方法如下：

long@pc3:/usr/local/lib/python2.7/dist-packages$ sudo passwd
Enter new UNIX password:
Retype new UNIX password:
passwd: password updated successfully
long@pc3:/usr/local/lib/python2.7/dist-packages$ su - root
Password:
root@pc3:~# ls
